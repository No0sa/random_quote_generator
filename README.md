# Random Quote Generator

A beautiful and inspiring Flutter application that provides your daily dose of wisdom through a collection of curated quotes from famous personalities.

## 🚀 Features

- **Daily Inspiration**: Get fresh quotes at the tap of a button to start your day with motivation.
- **Sleek Animations**: Enjoy a smooth experience with fade and slide transitions that make every quote feel special.
- **Premium Aesthetics**: Experience a modern, vibrant interface with deep indigo and purple gradients.
- **Easy Sharing**: Share your favorite quotes with friends or copy them to your clipboard instantly (UI actions).
- **Favorites**: Keep track of the quotes that resonate with you most (UI demonstration).
- **Responsive Design**: A fluid layout that looks great on any screen size.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/)
- **Language**: [Dart](https://dart.dev/)
- **State Management**: [Provider](https://pub.dev/packages/provider)
- **Animations**: Tween Animations & FadeTransitions

## 📦 Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following installed:
- [Flutter SDK](https://docs.flutter.dev/get-started/install)
- [Dart SDK](https://dart.dev/get-started/sdk_setup)
- An IDE (VS Code, Android Studio, or IntelliJ)
